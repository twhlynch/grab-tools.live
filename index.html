Grab Tools

Welcome to Grab Tools! The best collection of modding tools, resources, statistics and more for the VR game GRAB. Brought to you by .index.

Here you will find my JSON Level Editor, a combination of many tools for editing levels in JSON and 3D, and running premade functions to modify levels. You can use the cheat sheet developed by me and MiloJenSopha to generate specific levels with a list of all possible vanilla and modded objects. There are also a large amount of stats and leaderboards such as the unbeaten levels list, and most played levels list. We even have a wiki page hosted on miraheze.
